Food Intolerance Guide — Content Editor

Add, edit, or remove foods, categories, and traits below, then download a new foods-data.js and use it to replace the one in your project repo. This page isn't linked from the live site and doesn't change anything until you download and swap the file in yourself.

Traits

A trait is anything foods can share (FODMAPs, fiber, fat, a trait you invent). "Show as a filter checkbox" controls whether visitors can exclude it from the analysis on the live site. Changing a trait's ID updates it everywhere it's used.

Categories & Foods

Check the boxes for whichever traits each food has. Use the ID field if you want a specific internal name — otherwise the auto-generated one is fine to leave alone.

Articles

Load articles-data.js, edit raw content per article, download when done.
